Edit once, use anywhere.

A CV 2.0 is the next-generation resume. You fill out your data once and export it to the file formats of your choice. You don't have to register or even use your real data. We use the CV 2.0 - Global Resume W3C Community Group's specification for our .cv2files.

Privacy

The core functions are open sourced and your data is yours. We do not keep your files on our servers for more than 20 minutes. The option to sign up, keep your data, and use additional features will be added continuously.

Most of this site's functionality is still in early development - check our GitHub repository for news. For now, this site just stands for demonstration purposes.
